Easy Specification Tables Privacy Policy

Easy Specification Tables ("the App") provides functionality that allows merchants to add specification tables to their products ("the Service") for stores using Shopify to power their businesses. This Privacy Policy outlines how personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use the App in connection with your Shopify-supported store.

Personal Information the App Collects

When you install the App, we are automatically granted access to certain types of information from your Shopify account through the following Shopify API permissions: read_products, write_products, write_script_tags, and write_themes. This access facilitates the collection of basic store information and access to metafields which are used to display on shops.

We do not store this data permanently as part of our standard operations; however, we reserve the right to store this information temporarily, such as for caching purposes, if needed in the future.

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

The personal information we access from your Shopify account is used to provide and operate the App effectively. Additionally, we utilize this information for the following purposes:

  • Marketing: To communicate with you about product updates, new services, and promotional offers that may be of interest to you.
  • Analytics: To evaluate how the App is used, identifying areas for improvement and upgrade.
  • Support: To provide customer support and handle your inquiries efficiently.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We reserve the right to share your personal information with third parties if necessary to provide and optimize our Service. This may include analytics providers, marketing partners, and other service providers who assist us in operating the App. We will always ensure that these parties adhere to strict data protection and confidentiality terms equivalent to those laid out in this Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your store remains a customer of the App. Upon uninstallation of the App from your Shopify store, we receive a notification from Shopify and will adhere to Shopify's protocol to remove your information. However, we reserve the right to retain some data if required or permitted by law.

Your Rights

If you are a resident of certain jurisdictions, you may have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us through the contact information below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, new regulatory requirements, or other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
